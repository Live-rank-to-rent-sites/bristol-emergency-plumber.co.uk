Bristol Emergency Plumber

Professional emergency plumbing company serving Bristol and surrounding areas.

Company Overview

Bristol Emergency Plumber provides professional emergency and scheduled plumbing services to residential and commercial customers across Bristol and the surrounding area. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our services include emergency plumbing call-outs, boiler repair and servicing, blocked drain clearance, gas safety certificates, central heating repair, and burst pipe response. All gas work is carried out by Gas Safe registered engineers. We offer fixed pricing with no hidden extras.

Services Offered

  • Emergency Plumber – 24/7 emergency response for burst pipes, leaks, flooding, and urgent plumbing failures
  • Boiler Repair – Diagnosis and repair of all boiler makes and models, including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, and Ideal
  • Boiler Servicing – Annual boiler servicing to maintain efficiency and manufacturer warranty
  • Blocked Drains – Clearing blocked sinks, toilets, baths, showers, and external drains
  • Gas Safety Certificates – CP12 gas safety inspections for landlords, homeowners, and businesses
  • Central Heating Repair – Radiator repairs, power flushing, thermostat replacement, and system balancing

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to a plumbing emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency call-outs across Bristol and surrounding areas. Response times are typically same-day, with many emergencies attended within 1-2 hours.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Bristol, Clifton, Bath, Portishead, Clevedon, Nailsea, Keynsham, Yate, Thornbury, Bradley Stoke, Filton, and Winterbourne, plus surrounding areas.

Are your engineers Gas Safe registered?

Yes, all engineers carrying out gas work are fully Gas Safe registered. This is a legal requirement in the UK for work on gas appliances and pipework.

Do you provide gas safety certificates?

Yes, we issue CP12 gas safety certificates for landlords, homeowners, and businesses across Bristol and surrounding areas.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments, and bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of work.

Do you offer guarantees on your work?

Yes, all our plumbing work comes with a satisfaction guarantee. We stand behind the quality of our services.
